STRAIN · 7 DAYS

5 variants · hero untouched · one accent
No traffic-light, no WHOOP bars. Position carries the read: inside the corridor = matched (lime), below = under-reached, above = over-reached. Colour stays one accent.
V1 · INSTRUMENT LOG
Seven rows, one per day — each a mini scale with the target band and your marker. Reads like a control-room log.
matchedmissed (position = under / over)
V2 · DOT PLOT
The corridor as a band; each day a single dot at its load. In-band lights lime, misses sit above or below, dim.
V3 · MINI DIALS
Seven small dashed rings — the hero dial, repeated. Each fills to its load with the band marked. Most cohesive with the top.
V4 · TICK COLUMNS
The ring unrolled vertically — stacked dashes lit to the load, two bright ticks mark the band. Instrument, not a bar.
V5 · DEVIATION
The band is the centreline. A tick rises when you over-reached, drops when you under-reached; matched days rest as a flat lime node.
